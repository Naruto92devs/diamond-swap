The Basics

Price impact is the term used to describe the influence that a trade has on the value of a specific cryptocurrency. Specifically, it measures the degree to which the price of a cryptocurrency is altered due to a buy or sell action.

A tolerated loss of value

Traders have accepted 'price impact' as a tolerated loss in value, and often choose to split their sells up over time in an attempt to reduce it. However, this technique carries a risk of significant market price reductions between the trades, resulting in considerable loss of profits.

Trading without loss to price impact is a lot more profitable.

The loss to price impact often goes unnoticed. Once we start to realise how much we're losing with each trade, we start to look for ways to eliminate or reduce those losses.
